Mission

At Lipari Family Tutoring, we strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere in which all students are able to work through their confusion around math and science. We focus on developing not only the procedural fluency necessary to complete problems confidently, but also the underlying conceptual understanding that allows students to apply new ideas to unfamiliar problems. We also help students develop their study habits, highlighting the important concepts to continue practicing between tutoring sessions, and emailing important notes from our tutoring sessions when it would be helpful.
